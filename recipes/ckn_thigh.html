Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Description

If you've never made chicken thighs in the air fryer,

you need this top-rated air fryer chicken thigh recipe in your life ASAP.

The results are irresistible thighs that are tender and juicy on the inside,

but nice and crispy on the outside.

Ingredients

  • 4 skin-on boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
